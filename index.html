About Me

My name is Kaan. I am a senior Mechanical Engineering student at Katip Çelebi University. I have a passion for scientific research and engineering, particularly in the field of aerospace technologies. I aim to become a researcher and problem-solver who understands both science and its practical applications.

During my academic journey, I have worked on CFD simulations of a hydrodynamic journal bearing and on the film cooling of turbine blades. I am currently working remotely with CORET (Combustion Research Team) from Tarsus University, and I will also be a voluntary research internship at ODTÜ-GÜNAM (Center for Solar Energy Research and Applications) beginning 25 agust. If you want to learn more about my work, you can check the "Projects" section. Also here is my CV.

My goal is to be someone who understands physics, mathematics, and the fundamentals of engineering—someone who views the world like a scientist. I also enjoy doing calisthenics and reading. I might post a few of my writings as well.
